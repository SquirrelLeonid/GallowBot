Here is help for gallows game.
___|Rules|___
-You have 10 lives. By other words you can make a miss only 9 times, 10 will be a lose.
-If you guessed a right letter, then all the same letters will be opened in a word.
___|Commands|___
-gallows start [start a new game]
-gallows letter <letter> [try to guess a letter]
-gallows stop [Stop this game. Progress won't be saved]
-gallows help [Show this message]
